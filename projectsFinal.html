Poke-laces

An ongoing project of mine are pokemon cards that I turn into necklaces. I collect primarily Japanese cards and put them in a hard plastic scleeve. Following the art style and color of the card I use thrifted fabrics to decorate the card and finally string it up to be worn. I was heavily influenced by Mcowls a good friend of mine who takes mcdonald fry packages and uses spray paint to turn them into small art pieces that people wear at festivals. It has been a lot of fun finding fabric at thrift shops that fit the colorways of the cards perfectly, each necklace is a small visual puzzle I have to solve!
